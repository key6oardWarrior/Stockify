Vision

In today's world, there are various options available for personal wealth growth, including both IRAs, saving accounts, Mutual funds and more. However, many of these options have a great deal of risk associated with them, and their return are often not significant. For instance, while IRAs and saving account avoid the risk of stock market, they also have a very small return. On the other hand, mutual fund requires the investors to trust the fund managers expertise, which might not always lead to favorable outcomes. while the average return from a broker's IRA is around 4%. Congressperson on the other hand performs way better than IRAs saving accounts and mutual funds.

Solution

How can we solve this problem? We brainstormed on the ideal to create a app called STOKIFY. This app aims to cater to the need of people who are involved in the stock market by ensuring that their trades, calls, options and more are sound and more likely to succeed. Today, around 53% of Americans own stocks and this number is up from 1989, where only 32% of Americans owned stocks. It is evidence that Americans have a significant interest in stock market. This app is the business venture that can have a significant effect on millions of people.
